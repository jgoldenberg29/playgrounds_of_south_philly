Columbus Square Playground

Neighborhood

The playground is located between Wharton and Reed st between 12th and 13th st in South Philadelphia. In this neighborhood, known as Passyunk Sq, the playground is centrally located and accessible to thousands of families. It is frequented by the local elementry, preschool and daycares and a hub for socializing and community for parents and children alike.

Playground

Columbus square playground is a newly renovated and state of the art playground. there are three sections of the playground: The "Big kid Playground," the "tot-lot," and a water feature. The "tot lot" is conveniently enclosed so the younger children can play safely amongst themselves. There are swing sets, slides and climbing structures for all ages. And the water feature that runs from 11am - 7pm from memorial day to laborday lets the kids (and parents) cool off in the summer months.

Other Park Features

Columbus square park also has a new dog park divided for smaller and larger dogs. In addition to a sports field for the local recreational leagues. And it is surrounded by beautiful walking paths and picnic areas for family meals and activities.
